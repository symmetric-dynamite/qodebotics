Helpful answers

These are some of the most commonly asked questions.

How much do you charge?

The cost depends on the nature and scope of your project. Each project is different, and has different objectives, so we don't have fixed prices. Please contact us to discuss your project in more detail.

What are your areas of expertise?

We have expertise in developing blockchain applications, mobile applications, web applications, and IoT devices.

What programming languages do you use?

The scope of the project typically determines the programming language. Our programmers predominatly use C, C#, C++, Python, Ruby, PHP, Golang, Javascript, Java, Swift and Kotlin.

Do you charge a monthly retainer fee

No, we do not. If you need us to maintain the site, update code, or edit content, then you can choose our low cost hourly rate or negotiate a fixed rate.

How much are the server costs?

There are a number of companies such as bluehost.com that provide server space for static websites. These websites typically charge less than $5 per month. If you are building a mobile or web application, we would advise using AWS or Microsoft Azure cloud servers since they are highly scalable. Our development team will be happy to discuss these options in more detail.

I already own a website. Can I change programmers?

If you are unhappy with your current developer, then we are happy to help. Let's first have a conversation about the scope of your project. If it's something we can help with, then we'll review the coding files. If you don't have the coding files, then you will need to request them from your programmer.
